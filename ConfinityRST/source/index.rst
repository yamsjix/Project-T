Overview of C-LLM
=========================================

Confinity Solutions Low Latency Messaging (LLM) is a product designed for the
high-performance messaging market space that is characterized by exceptionally
high message volumes combined with sub-millisecond latency requirements. The
current version of C-LLM is designed primarily for the messaging requirements of
the front-office of financial institutions.

Although announced as a product in November 2007, LLM has been in
development for several years prior as the Reliable Multicast Messaging (RMM)
project from the IBM Haifa Research Lab. LLM is branded as part of the IBM
messaging family of transports.
